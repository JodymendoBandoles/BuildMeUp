About Us

Unleashing Potential, Inspiring Futures: Discover the
Story of BuildMeUp

Welcome to BuildMeUp, Welcome to BuildMeUp, a place where dreams become reality and young minds are empowered to reach their full potential. Founded by Esmeralda Bandoles, a visionary educator with a passion for nurturing young learners, BuildMeUp School has been transforming lives for the past five years. At BuildMeUp, we believe that education is the key to unlocking endless possibilities. Esmeralda Bandoles' long-standing dream of creating a school that provides quality education and holistic development has become a reality. With a dedicated team of educators and a supportive community, BuildMeUp has grown exponentially, expanding its influence throughout Ormoc City. Our mission is to inspire and ignite a love for learning in every child. We strive to create an environment that fosters creativity, curiosity, and critical thinking. Through innovative teaching methods, personalized attention, and a diverse range of educational programs, we aim to equip our students with the skills and knowledge they need to thrive in a rapidly changing world. At BuildMeUp, we go beyond academics. We nurture the social, emotional, and physical well-being of our students, providing a holistic approach to education. Our state-of-the-art facilities, interactive learning spaces, and engaging extracurricular activities create a vibrant and dynamic learning environment. As we continue to grow, we remain committed to our core values of excellence, integrity, and community. BuildMeUp School is not just a place of education; it is a community of passionate learners, dedicated teachers, and supportive parents, all working together to shape the future leaders of tomorrow. Join us on this exciting journey of unleashing potential and inspiring futures. Together, let's build a brighter tomorrow at BuildMeUp School. Welcome to BuildMeUp, where dreams take flight and the future is shaped.

Pioneering Excellence: Meet Esmeralda Bandoles,

Esmeralda Bandoles, the visionary founder of BuildMeUp, has brought her long-standing dream of creating an exceptional educational institution to life. With her unwavering commitment and passion for early childhood education, Esmeralda has paved the way for a nurturing and stimulating learning environment. Her innovative approach and relentless pursuit of excellence have established BuildMeUp as a pioneering institution, dedicated to empowering young learners and shaping their bright futures.

 Meet Our Exceptional Team of Educators 

Shaping Young Minds with Passion and Expertise

 Teacher-Maria 

Inspiring Minds, Nurturing Language Skills. With honors as Magna Cum Laude from Philippine Normal University, Maria is a highly accomplished educator specializing in early development education. As an English teacher at BuildMeUp Learning Center, she brings expertise, compassion, and a nurturing approach. Maria creates a supportive environment where students thrive, developing strong language skills. Her commitment to quality education makes her an invaluable asset to the BuildMeUp learning community.

 Meet Teacher Emily 

Fueling Passion for Sports, Shaping Young Athletes. As the Sports Coordinator at BuildMeUp Learning Center, Emily Dalisay ignites a love for physical activity and empowers young athletes. With expertise in diverse sports disciplines, she fosters discipline, teamwork, and a healthy lifestyle. Emily's dedication inspires students to excel in sports and develop essential life skills. Her unwavering commitment makes her an invaluable asset to the BuildMeUp community.

 Meet Teacher Janice 

Guiding Future Innovators. With her engaging teaching style and commitment to fostering scientific curiosity, Janice empowers students to explore, experiment, and discover the wonders of the world around them. Her dedication to nurturing young minds makes her an invaluable asset to the BuildMeUp learning community.
